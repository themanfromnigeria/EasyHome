How to Hire Artisans on EasyHome with just a click

STEP 1

Tell us what you need

Submit your request by telling us what you need in a few steps. Share your requirements, preferences, and any specific details about the task, so we can match you with the best artisan around you. It's simple and free.

STEP 2

We will arrange an Inspection (Optional)

After submitting your request, we'll promptly schedule an inspection at your convenience if required. Our team will visit your location to assess the task firsthand and ensure we fully understand your needs.

STEP 3

Receive a Quote

After the inspection is completed, we will send you detailed quotes within hours with an accessible link to the profile of the top recommended artisan for the job. This quote will encompass all relevant aspects of the job.

STEP 4

Choose the Best One

Compare the ratings and availability of each artisan and pick the best. On occasions where you don't know which to pick, EasyHome supports you with advice, cost estimations, and contractor ratings from our users.
